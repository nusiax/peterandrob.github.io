Kampen is the place I have the best memories of. It was the place where I was growing from a little boy into a teenager, a very important time in life.  The time one becomes aware
of the facts of life. But it was also the time I sadly lost my mother when she died in 1968.
The city of Kampen is still as beautiful now, as I remember it from the sixties. In 2002 Rob and I went there for a visit and to me it still holds a lot of nostalgia, I felt like being home.
I lived in Kampen for 5 years from 1964 until 1969 when we
moved to Middelburg, due to my father's last transfer in his
millitary life.
