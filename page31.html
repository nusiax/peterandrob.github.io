In 1962 we moved from Nunspeet to Delfzijl, a town in the north-east of the country in the province of Groningen.
We lived there for about a year and a half, too short to really settle down, let alone to adapt to the new schools my brother and I had to go to. It was also too short to memorize it very well.
What I do remember of Delfzijl is that I did like living there and spending a lot of time on rollerskating in the streets. The people in the province of Groningen spoke a Groningen dialect, but it was amazing to find out that as a child at the age of 9 or 10 years old, how quick you pick up the dialect and thus understand it. It was so very different to where we came from.........
