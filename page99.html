From 1980 - 1986 I was president of The Official Dutch Diana Ross Fan Club. For five years I have been publishing a quarterly fan club magazine called "Diana" and the fan club saw its number of members go up to a solid 800 and the members came from all over the world! At first the fan club magazine had a seperate Dutch and English version, but after the first two years, the magazine was bilingual. Running the fan club was absolutely wonderful and I had the opportunity to meet some wonderful people!!! The last year I finished running the fan club because of the lack of support from the Diana Ross management which caused me not to be motivated anymore.
