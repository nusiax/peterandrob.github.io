After leaving my parents' home in 1972, I first moved to a furnished room for about a year (Houtkaai 3) and then moved to an apartment in a block, bedore moving to Vlissingen.
Over the years I have moved between Middelburg and Vlissingen a couple of times but lived in Middelburg most of the time. I also lived in two other houses in Middelburg: first at Nieuwepoortstraat 1 and then Segeerssingel 146 which was also the last house I lived in, in Middelburg. Afterwards I moved to Vlissingen in 1994 and stayed there until moving to Amsterdam in December 2002.
